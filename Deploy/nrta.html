Northrock Rail Transport Agency

The NRTA was established to help guide Northrock's rail offering and provide future rail transport to northrock and the surrounding region. As Nerd.NU's only privately managed rail provider the NRTA is proud to be able to provide service for years to come.

NRTA JUMP

JUMP into transport with a NRTA JUMP card availible from Northrock Central Station
JUMP will allow you to jump on and jump off trains without worrying about a ticket just simply top up online or in station its as easy as that!

NRTA Yellow Line Station

Your gateway to Northrock

The NRTA Yellow Line Station is the gateway to Northrock this station connects to the Northrock Main Line and takes you directly into the heart of Northrock.
NRTA Yellow is a small station consisting of a departures and arrivals area and parking.

NRTA Yellow What You Need To Know

NRTA Yellow is a ticketless station this means you will need to be a NRTA JUMP Cardholder or pre book your ticket online via our NRTA Ticketing Site.

To get to NRTA Yellow take the CTA Yellow line north. You will see the NRTA Yellow Station while riding there is no CTA station so be ready to jump!

NRTA Central Station

Northrock's biggest station.

Connecting Northrock to the world NRTA Central has it all!
Grab your NRTA JUMP card at our front desk or take a break at our local coffee shop before taking a ride on our service to CTA Yellow.

NRTA Central What You Need To Know

This station requires pre booked tickets (availible from the front desk) or a NRTA JUMP card

This station currently connects to the NRTA main line.
