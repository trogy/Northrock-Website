Welcome to the NRTA

Thank you for your interest in connecting to our extensive rail network below you will find guidelines and rules

NRTA Rail Lines - Main Requirements for Buildings

- All stations must not disrupt or move the rail line away from its original course.

- All Stations are required to have a NRTA JUMP terminal installed (You can go about this however you like just any kind of machine looking thing that says something along the lines of insert ticket.)

- Stations may run other non NRTA lines out of them, these lines must not interfere in any way with NRTA lines.

- You may build bridges level crossings and tunnels under and over the NRTA lines bridges must be AT LEAST 5 blocks above and tunnels AT LEAST 3 blocks below level crossings must be built to a reasonable standard use the single crossing in Northrock town center for reference (You dont have to follow the level crossing design exactly).
Failure to comply with these rules will result in the removal of stop points at your station and/or removal of the station from within NRTA boundaries
A warning will be givin prior to removal of anything.

Please add a sign with your username somewhere in your station so we can contact you.

For line specific Requirements scroll down

NRTA North Eastern Special Requirements

- Connecting a station at any point along the hill bridge is prohibited

-Building directly above the hill bridge is prohibited

-Building Below the hill bridge is allowed

NRTA Scenic Railway

- You must not in any way tamper with or move rails one this line

-You must not add stations to this line

-Permission to build level crossings bridges and tunnels is available from djtrogy in game.
