NRTA North Eastern Railway Under Construction

The NRTA is pleased to announce the NRTA North Eastern Railway this is set to be a public rail with a hub in Northrock's Main Station. Find out rules for building along this route by clicking the link above.

VIVO SPK and TruckersFM setup offices in Northrock

Northrock city council has welcomed new buildings from companies SPK TruckersFM and VIVO these companies have long runnning histories in Telecoms, Financial and Radio and will be a great addition to our growing city.

NRTA Scenic Rail Finished

The NRTA's Scenic rail has been completed featuring stunning from the top of the nearby mountains this railway is set to bring in large amounts of tourism.

Northrock Rail Transport Agency Established

The Northrock city council has established the Northrock Rail Transport Agency to oversee current and future rail transport into and out of Northrock.
